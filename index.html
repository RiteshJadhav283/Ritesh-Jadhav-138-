Q1. What is the difference between padding and margin in CSS?
a. Padding - Padding Creates the space in the element and the border. Syntax : padding:(values). There are left and top padding also
b. Margin - Creates the space outside the border. Syntax: Margin: (values)

Q2. How does font-family influence the look of a webpage?.
Font family it used in style to change the font of the entire tag. Example p style="font family: arial". This will change the font of the element throught the current p tag.
